Rainbow Publications is committed to advancing academic research by offering exceptional editorial support, publishing solutions, and safeguarding intellectual property rights. We operate with integrity, strive for excellence, and embrace innovation in every service we provide.

  • Thesis & Dissertation Support: Expert guidance, editing, and writing assistance.
  • Journal Submission Assistance: Help in selecting journals and managing the submission process.
  • Book Publication Services: Comprehensive editorial, design, and promotion solutions.
  • Intellectual Property Services: Assistance with patents, copyrights, and design registrations.
  • Academic Writing Services: Research proposals, review papers, editing, and proofreading support.
  • Plagiarism Checking: Industry-standard software to ensure originality and authenticity.
  • Conference Proceedings Publication: Publication in peer-reviewed journals for conference papers.

We guarantee complete confidentiality of all client details, research documents, and manuscripts entrusted to us. No information is shared with third parties without explicit written approval.

We adhere to stringent ethical and academic publishing practices, guided by experienced editors across disciplines, following accepted style guides and industry best practices to ensure precision and credibility.

Authors retain full copyright of their work. Any permissions required for third-party materials are the responsibility of the author.

We maintain a strict no-plagiarism policy. All submitted works are screened using advanced plagiarism detection tools. Any work found to be plagiarized will be immediately rejected.

We offer clear and transparent pricing with detailed quotations provided before service commencement. Flexible payment options are available for clients.

Refunds are available under specific terms. We advise clients to carefully review our refund and cancellation policy before engaging our services.

While we aim for accuracy in our services and publications, Rainbow Publications is not liable for any damages resulting from their use.

Any disputes arising will be resolved amicably through mediation or arbitration, following Indian legal frameworks.

Our policies are subject to change at any time, with significant updates communicated to clients accordingly.
